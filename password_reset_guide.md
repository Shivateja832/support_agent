# Password Reset and Account Access Guide

## Password Reset Process

### Standard Password Reset
1. Go to the login page at app.cloudsync.io
2. Click "Forgot Password" below the login form
3. Enter your registered email address
4. Check your email for a reset link (arrives within 2 minutes)
5. Click the link — it expires in 24 hours
6. Enter your new password (minimum 12 characters, must include uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character)
7. Confirm the new password
8. Log in with your new credentials

### If You Don't Receive the Reset Email
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Ensure you're checking the correct email account
- Add noreply@cloudsync.io to your safe senders list
- Wait 5 minutes before requesting another reset link
- Contact support if issue persists after 10 minutes

## Account Lockout

### Why Accounts Get Locked
- 5 consecutive failed login attempts
- Suspicious activity detected
- Admin-enforced lock
- Password reset initiated but not completed within 24 hours

### Unlocking Your Account
**Self-Service (Temporary Lock):**
Wait 15 minutes — temporary locks auto-expire after 3 failed attempt cycles.

**Permanent Lock:**
Contact support via:
- Email: support@cloudsync.io
- Phone: 1-800-CLOUDSYNC (business hours)
- Live chat in the Help Center

**Required Information for Account Unlock:**
- Account email address
- Last 4 digits of the credit card on file OR
- Two-factor authentication backup code
- Date of last successful login (approximate)

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

### Setting Up 2FA
1. Go to Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication
2. Choose your method: Authenticator app, SMS, or hardware key
3. Follow the setup wizard
4. Save your 10 backup codes in a secure location

### Lost 2FA Access
If you've lost access to your authenticator:
1. Use one of your saved backup codes at login
2. If backup codes are also lost, contact support with identity verification

### Backup Codes
- Each backup code can only be used once
- Generate new codes at Settings → Security → Backup Codes
- Old codes are invalidated when new ones are generated

## Social Login Issues

### Google Login Not Working
- Ensure the Google account matches your CloudSync registration email
- Clear browser cookies and try again
- Check if Google account has 2FA that may be blocking access
- Try linking accounts at Settings → Connected Accounts

### Microsoft/Azure AD Login
- Contact your organization's IT admin if SSO fails
- Verify your account isn't in a restricted group
- Check if your organization's CloudSync subscription is active

## Account Recovery
If all self-service options fail:
1. Contact support with government-issued ID
2. Provide business verification if using a company account
3. Recovery takes 1-3 business days for security verification
4. Enterprise customers have dedicated account recovery specialists

## Password Policy
- Minimum 12 characters
- Must include: uppercase, lowercase, number, special character (!@#$%^&*)
- Cannot be the same as last 5 passwords
- Must be changed every 180 days (Enterprise policy)
- Password manager integration recommended (1Password, Bitwarden, LastPass)
